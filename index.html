Who I am

Why Software Development?

My journey into software development began with a curiosity about building tools that simplify life. I have a strong interest in how human psychology can improve these tools, leading me to focus on cognitive sciences as a long term career goal. I thrive on the challenge of programming, experiencing euphoria when solving difficult problems.

Balancing my studies with my personal life, I enjoy painting and I believe my creativity and unique approach to programming set me apart. I emphasize the importance of community and collaboration in development, believing that sharing ideas and feedback drives innovation. Motivated by my career goals, I am eager to practice extensively, and immerse myself in my learning and growth.

I am a proud student at Codespace Academy where I am developing strong skills in fullstack development as I learn HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and various programming paradigms such as Object Oriented Programming and Functional Programming. My experience includes building dynamic websites and applications and debugging.
